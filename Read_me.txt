This project is a simple implementation of Augmented Reality using OpenCV.
In this project to implementation it several procedure are being processed.
The targeted picture and a video that is to be augment to the targeted image are being fetched.
The featured points are being detected from the image and match with the visuals of the camera.
The visuals from the camera are being wrapped with the video that has been fetched earlier and
then with the help of masking the visual the video is being augmented to the matched targeted image.
